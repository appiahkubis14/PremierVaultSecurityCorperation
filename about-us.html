You can rest well knowing your precious metals are stored with Premier Vault Security Company. We offer the safest and most cost effective gold and silver vault storage in the industry, with 24 hour online access and our legendary customer service.

Through strategic partnerships with the world’s top institutional vault providers, our customers can attain fully-insured and segregated gold and silver storage around the world, at prices far below the alternatives
With our segregated storage:

  • Metals are held solely in your name (or a joint account, trust, LLC or other option you choose).
  • The exact bullion you buy is always stored separately, and independently audited and verified – no pooling or shared ownership, just your metals in your title.
  • Customers receive regular custody certificates documenting their holdings and account inventory levels.
  • Your holdings are a secure phone call or a few clicks away from door delivery or a sellback directly from storage.
